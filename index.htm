Product

hirecloud.pro (hire, cloud pro) is a cloud based telecommuting platform offering Job Portal with Interview Management System, Virtual Desktops (via IaaS and SaaS) and Conferencing facilities for Startups, SMEs, Enterprises looking to hire telecommuting professionals, placed all over the world. Our solution is the first and only true TCaaS (Telecommuting as a Service) offering. Please refer to our blogs for various use cases where hirecloud.pro can help you.


About

hireCloud.pro (hire, cloud pro) is a flagship cloud based web application developed by Kudupudi Systems LLP. The company is founded by Ugendreshwar Kudupudi who is from Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India.

Being a big believer in smart cities concept, hirecloud.pro aim is to allow professionals from various countries, cities and villages to work from home instead of moving to bigger cities or countries and at the same time work for leading startups or enterprises without loosing out on opportunities.

Our mission is to allow people to connect, work and collaborate without borders.
